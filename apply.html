Explore Our Courses

Yale School of Art offers diverse programs to nurture creative talents and foster artistic innovation. Below are the primary courses available:

  • MFA in Painting/Printmaking

    Explore cutting-edge techniques in painting and printmaking, emphasizing creative experimentation, conceptual exploration, and critical thinking to develop a deeper understanding of artistic processes and contemporary visual practices.

  • MFA in Sculpture

    Enhance your sculptural practice by exploring both innovative and traditional techniques, utilizing our state-of-the-art studios to push creative boundaries and refine your skills in a dynamic, hands-on environment.

  • MFA in Graphic Design

    Cultivate advanced skills in visual communication, digital media, and print design, mastering techniques to create visually compelling and impactful designs that effectively convey messages across diverse platforms and audiences.

  • MFA in Photography

    Hone your photographic skills through courses that focus on technical expertise, while exploring contemporary practices and creative approaches to capture compelling images and push the boundaries of visual storytelling.

Entry Requirements

Before applying to Yale School of Art, ensure you meet the following requirements:

  • A bachelor’s degree from an accredited institution or equivalent qualification.
  • A strong portfolio showcasing your artistic abilities and creativity.
  • A completed application form, including a personal statement.
  • Two letters of recommendation from professionals or academics.
  • English language proficiency for non-native speakers (TOEFL/IELTS).

Meeting these requirements ensures your application is eligible for review by our admissions committee.
